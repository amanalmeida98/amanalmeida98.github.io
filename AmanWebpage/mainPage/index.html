Services

Production

I work as a producer with many songwriters to develop their demos into full-bodied songs. Through these projects, I’ve learned the essential skills of music production as well as project management.

Teaching

Personal satisfaction from helping others understand music is what drives me as a teacher. I’ve taught students as young as 7 and as old as 60 years old, including some students with learning disabilities. I aspire to teach piano and music production at a collegiate level in the future.

Sound Design

Growing up, I always envisioned myself working in the film industry – but I ended up pursuing a degree in music. Sound design is the perfect excuse for me to once again rub shoulders with filmmakers. Designing the perfect sounds for a film is a chase that I live for, and I go deeper into it with every project.

Performing

I have played the piano for over 10 years, and have been fortunate enough to share the stage with many artists I admire. Jazz, Neo-soul, Classical and Bossa Nova are my absolute favorite styles to play, but I am no stranger to playing Dream Pop in a dimly lit basement. Through experience playing as an aux keyboardist, I’ve learned my way around various lines of keyboards/synthesizers including Nords, the MicroKORG, the MicroBrute, Sequential Prophets, various Juno synths and of course the Fender Rhodes.
